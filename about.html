    This is the least album that I listened to. I guess the reason behind this is that I added these songs to my playlist after the others. So while I was smashing the replay button above, these songs didn't get much chance.

    The album was released independently on September 27, 2006 at NEMeadow Studios in Bath. Then it was mastered by John Walsh at Lava Room Recordings in Cleveland.
